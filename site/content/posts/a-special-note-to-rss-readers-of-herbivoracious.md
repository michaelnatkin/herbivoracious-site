Thank you for being a regular reader of Herbivoracious via the RSS feed! As I'm sure you know by now, Google is shutting down Google Reader on July 1. You can of course choose a new RSS reader and continue to subscribe to the <a href="/feed/" target="_blank">feed</a> there. An even easier option is to subscribe via email. If you fill out the form below, I'll make sure you get each new recipe in your inbox. (If the form doesn't work for you, <a href="http://forms.aweber.com/form/75/1783892175.htm" target="_blank">click here to sign up</a>.)

Thanks,
Michael
