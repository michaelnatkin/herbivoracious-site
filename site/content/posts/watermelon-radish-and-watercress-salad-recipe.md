<p><strong>Watermelon Radish and Watercress Salad</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>1 large watermelon radish, very thinly sliced</li>
<li>extra virgin olive oil</li>
<li>4 handfuls fresh watercress, rinsed and dried</li>
<li>1 lemon</li>
<li>4 ripe fresh figs, halved</li>
<li>¼ cup pomegranate arils</li>
<li>16 shavings of Parmgiano-Reggiano or other hard aged cheese</li>
<li>12 <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B08KRC5Q4M?tag=herb-hugo-20">toasted walnut halves</a></li>
<li><a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00017028M?tag=herb-hugo-20">flaky sea salt</a></li>
</ul>
<ol>
<li>Arrange the slices of watermelon radish in a circle on 4 chlled plates. Drizzle with a little olive oil.</li>
<li>Toss the watercress with a bit of the olive oil and a squeeze of lemon juice. Place a fluffy handful on each plate. This is the critical step to making the salad look nice – aim to make a tall, high mound in the center, don’t let it spread out.</li>
<li>Add two fig halves, a tablespoon of pomegranate arils, 4 shavings of gouda, and 3 toasted walnut halves to each plate. Serve immediately.</li>
</ol>
