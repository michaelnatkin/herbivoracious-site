Which I guess makes them a bit of an upstart on the Roman scene. I found them after a morning of strolling around <a href="/2007/10/campo-dei-fiori/">Campo dei Fiori</a> and my only regret was I only had room for the stellar pine-nut and raisin strudel with deliciously moist breadcrumb filling.
