<p><strong>Sauteed Corn with Lemongrass</strong>
</p>
<ul>
<li>3 tablespoons vegetable oil</li>
<li>1/2 red onion, finely diced</li>
<li>2 stalks lemongrass, tender parts only, minced</li>
<li>1 inch piece of fresh ginger, peeled and minced</li>
<li>3 cloves garlic, minced</li>
<li>2 <a href="https://www.amazon.com/dp/B00TZ8BK5W?tag=herb-hugo-20">kaffir lime leaves</a> (optional)</li>
<li>2 or more small hot green chile peppers, minced (optional)</li>
<li>2 medium yellow summer squash or zucchini, 1/2" dice</li>
<li>1 yellow bell pepper, 1/2" dice</li>
<li>zest of one lime</li>
<li>kernels from 5 ears sweet corn, removed from cob</li>
<li>1 teaspoon kosher salt</li>
<li>juice of 2 limes</li>
<li>1 Â½ cups cherry tomatoes, halved</li>
<li>3 green onions, white parts only, thinly sliced</li>
<li>fresh ground black pepper</li>
</ul>
<ol>
<li>Place a wok or a large skillet over very high heat. When pre-heated, add the vegetable oil, onion, lemongrass, ginger, garlic, and the optional kaffir lime leaves and chile pepper. Stir-fry for one minute.</li>
<li>Add the squash, bell pepper and lime zest and saute for 1 minute. Add the corn and salt and saute until tender, about 2-3 minutes.</li>
<li>Add the lime juice and turn off the heat. Adjust seasoning and lime juice as needed. Remove the kaffir lime leaves.</li>
<li>Garnish with the cherry tomatoes and green onions, a grind of fresh black pepper, and a few flakes of sea salt, and serve immediately.</li>
</ol>
