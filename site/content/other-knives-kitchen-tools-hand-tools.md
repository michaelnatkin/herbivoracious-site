<p>Besides your <a href="/chefs-knife-kitchen-tools-hand-tools/">chef's knife</a>, you’ll want to have a small paring knife and a good serrated bread knife. If you were to add just one more knife, especially for vegetarian cooking, I would make it a “tomato knife,” which is a little larger than a paring knife but serrated to slice easily through the tough skins of tomatoes, grapes, and the like.</p>
